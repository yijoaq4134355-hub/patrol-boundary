关于2025年正月十四巡境活动路线安排的通知 Notice on the Route Arrangement for the "Xunjing" Activity on the 14th Day of the First Lunar Month, 2025

各村民小组、相关农户： To all village groups and relevant households:

为切实保障本村传统民俗活动"巡境"安全有序开展，经村委会研究并报镇文化站备案，现将2025年正月十四巡境活动路线及相关事项通知如下： To ensure the safe and orderly execution of our village's traditional folk activity "Xunjing", after deliberation by the Village Committee and filing with the Town Culture Station, the route arrangement and relevant matters for the 2025 activity on the 14th day of the first lunar month are hereby notified as follows:

一、活动基本信息 I. Basic Activity Information

  • 活动名称：鹤田村2025年正月十四传统巡境仪式 Activity Name: Hetian Village 2025 Traditional Xunjing Ceremony
  • 活动时间：农历正月十四（公历2025年2月11日），上午8:00至下午17:00 Time: 14th day of the first lunar month (February 11, 2025), 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM
  • 主办单位：鹤田村传统文化保护小组 Organizer: Hetian Village Traditional Culture Protection Group
  • 审批单位：鹤田村村民委员会 Approving Authority: Hetian Village Committee
  • 安全责任人：周建国（村委文书），联系电话：138****4419 Safety Officer: Zhou Jianguo (Village Committee Clerk), Tel: 138****4419

二、巡境路线安排 II. Route Arrangement

经实地勘察，综合考虑交通状况、施工区域及人员聚集安全，确定本次巡境路线如下： Following an on-site survey and comprehensive consideration of traffic conditions, construction areas, and crowd safety, the Xunjing route is determined as follows:

序号No. 途经节点Route Node 预计停留时间Estimated Stay 备注Remarks
1 陈氏宗祠（起点）Chen Ancestral Hall (Start) 30分钟30 mins 举行起驾仪式，燃放鞭炮需提前报备Starting ceremony; firecrackers require prior reporting
2 东街East Street 20分钟20 mins 途经主干道，需协调交通管制Main road; traffic control coordination required
3 古井遗址Ancient Well Ruins 15分钟15 mins 举行净水仪式，注意台阶防滑Water purification ceremony; caution slippery steps
4 北坡临时祭点North Slope Temporary Altar 25分钟25 mins 因北坡道路施工，原定点位调整至此，详见附件Adjusted due to road construction; see attachment
5 旧小学旧址Old Primary School Ruins 15分钟15 mins 墙面老化，禁止倚靠Aging walls; leaning strictly prohibited
6 陈氏宗祠（终点）Chen Ancestral Hall (End) 30分钟30 mins 举行安座仪式，完成闭环Seating ceremony; route completion

三、路线调整说明 III. Route Adjustment Explanation

因北坡区域正在进行道路拓宽施工（工程编号：HT-2024-RD-07），原定于北坡神坛的停驻点无法正常使用。经与施工方协商，临时增设"北坡临时祭点"作为替代方案。该点位位于施工围挡外侧空地，距原址约42米，已落实以下安全措施： Due to the road widening construction in the North Slope area (Project No.: HT-2024-RD-07), the original altar cannot be used. After coordination with the construction party, a "North Slope Temporary Altar" is added as an alternative. It is located outside the construction fence, about 42 meters from the original site. The following safety measures have been implemented:

  1. 施工区域与活动区域设置硬质隔离，悬挂警示标识； Hard isolation is set up between construction and activity areas, with warning signs displayed;
  2. 临时祭点底座采用加固配重设计（总重约72公斤，四人协助搬运），确保防风稳固； The temporary altar base uses a reinforced counterweight design (total weight approx. 72kg, requiring four people to carry) to ensure wind resistance;
  3. 活动当日6:00-18:00，施工机械暂停作业。 Construction machinery will pause operations from 6:00 to 18:00 on the day of the activity.

四、工作要求 IV. Work Requirements

  1. 安全责任：各节点负责人须提前30分钟到位，检查消防设施及疏散通道； Safety Responsibility: Node personnel must arrive 30 mins early to check fire facilities and evacuation routes;
  2. 交通管控：东街段活动期间实行临时限行，请相关农户提前规划出行； Traffic Control: Temporary restrictions will be implemented on East Street; please plan your travel accordingly;
  3. 环境保护：严禁在排水渠遗址周边（含已填埋路段）燃放鞭炮或倾倒祭祀废弃物； Environmental Protection: Lighting firecrackers or dumping ritual waste near the drainage ditch ruins is strictly prohibited;
  4. 应急联络：如遇突发情况，立即联系现场总指挥周建国。 Emergency Contact: Contact the on-site commander, Zhou Jianguo, immediately in case of any emergencies.
